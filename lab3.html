Steps to make a cup of Tea

  1. Heat fresh water in a kettle or pot until it reaches boiling point.
  2. Put one tea bag into your mug or cup.
  3. Carefully pour the boiling water over the tea bag in the cup.
  4. Let it steep for 2–5 minutes, depending on how strong you like it
  5. Take the tea bag out and gently squeeze it against the side of the cup if you like stronger flavor.
  6. Add milk, sugar, honey, or lemon as desired. Stir well.
  7. Enjoy Your Tea! ☕
